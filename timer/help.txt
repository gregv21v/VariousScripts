exit         -- exits the program and updates your log file
             -- before you exit you will have the opportunity
             -- to split your time among multiple log files
             -- if your repo has multiple users.
check        -- checks the amount of time you have for this session
restart      -- restarts the timer from the beginning again
takebreak    -- pauses your session so you can take a break
commit (NYI) -- commits to your repo, you may need to setup
                 your login credentials in a separate file.
                 Make sure to keep them to yourself, and
                 don't tell anyone.
